About Us

Swiftlink is a non-profit organization that provides a fast and novel access to distnct web-links directed to Global Companies and Brands.

The aim is to help individuals, firms, companies, industries and organizations alleviate the friction and stress of having to randomly search for such links. With deligent research and analysis SwiftLink was able to source for vast links to distinct Global Companies and Brands, thus bringing them together in one space for quick access.
To make things more robust. SwiftLink is tirelessly working towards integration of a unique feature - a feature that enables users to add custom-link. This project was inspired after completing the Foundation stage of an intensive Software engineering program sponsored by Alx.
